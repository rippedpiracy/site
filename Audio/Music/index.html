Music

Websites

lucida - Music Downloader for Amazon Music, Soundcloud, Qobuz, Deezer and Tidal.

cobalt | community instances - The best looking and performing Online Downloader for Social Media (YouTube included).
Use a community instance with a high score.

RuTracker - Russian Tracker with a lot of Unknown or Foreign Music.
Requires Sign-up.

SoftArchive - SoftArchive's Music Section, has many different Albums you might not find elsewhere.

KHInsider - Offers plenty of Video Game Soundtracks and Game audio rips. Audio quality tends to be extremely low.

Sitting on Clouds | Sukidesuost | Nyaa - Very good and reliable sources to get mainly Anisons from, but also other Asian Music as well. Sitting on Clouds also has a good collection of Game OST. They use friendly file hosters except Nyaa, it uses Torrents instead.
AnimeThemes | Themes.moe - A huge collection of Ending/Opening Themes for Anime. Right click to download the Audio/Video.

Squidify - A Web Player for Game OSTs/Anisons/Japanese Music.
You can also stream locally, more info at https://wotaku.wiki/guides/music/squidify

Tools

SoulseekQT - P2P Music Sharing Client, can have some niche and hard to find Music.
        Nicotine+ - A graphical client for Soulseek that is frequently updated, unlike Soulseek and has added features.

Qobuz-DL - Qobuz downloader. Supports downloads of last.fm, Spotify and Apple Music playlists through Qobuz.
Requires a paid Qobuz account.

Tidal-Media-Downloader - Tidal downloader in CLI. General guide for installation (guide for Tidal-gui may be outdated).
        Tidal-Media-Downloader-PRO - GUI version.
Both require a paid Tidal account.

SCDL - SoundCloud Music downloader, can set id3tags to downloaded Music too.

streamrip - A Scriptable Music downloader for Qobuz, Tidal, SoundCloud, and Deezer. Supports downloads of Spotify and Apple Music playlists through last.fm.
Requires a paid Qobuz/Tidal/Deezer account.
OrpheusDL - Music downloading client.
All plugins listed below require a paid account in their respective platforms except Musixmatch.
        OrpheusDL-Tidal - Tidal downloader plugin.
        OrpheusDL-Qobuz - Qobuz downloader plugin.
        OrpheusDL-Deezer - Deezer downloader plugin.
        OrpheusDL-SoundCloud - SoundCloud downloader plugin.
        OrpheusDL-Napster - Napster downloader plugin.
        OrpheusDL-Musixmatch - Musixmatch Lyrics downloader plugin.
        OrpheusDL-KKBox - KKBox downloader plugin.

spotDL - Download your Spotify playlists and songs along with album art and metadata fetched from YouTube.
This tool does not download songs directly from Spotify.

SpotX - Block video, audio and banner ads on Spotify, also unlocks some Premium and Experimental features.
        SpotX-Bash - Linux/Mac version.
BlockTheSpot - Video, audio & banner adblock/skip for Spotify on Windows.

YT-DLP - Can download from over 1500 sites including: Crunchyroll, Funimation, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, PornHub and more. Link to all supported sites.
Actively maintained fork of the popular YouTube Downloader youtube-dl with more functionality.

dzunlock - A browser UserScript that allows up to Hi-Fi streaming on Deezer.

Communities

SquidBoard - A forum for sharing and requesting any type of music including Game OSTs, Anime OSTs, TV music and more.
Requires Sign-up.

Doujin Café - The source of nearly all doujin rips online.
Discord Server.

r/xTrill - A Subreddit for requesting and sharing Music.

r/MusicHoarder - A SubReddit for general Music enthusiasts, they allow requesting and sharing Music. Check their Wiki for some useful information.

Misc

Firehawk52 Guide - A Mega-Guide for tools and ARLs to rip Music from popular streaming services.
THE MEGA MUSIC RIPPING GUIDE - A Music ripping guide for various platforms by Esoteria Avenue.

Wotaku-Music - Comprehensive information about audio files, hardware and Japanese Music.

Spek - Cross-platform GUI spectral creation program with easy drag and drop. Beginner-friendly but spectograms are not as detailed/clear as SoX.

SoX - Cross-platform CLI Swiss Army knife of sound processing programs, generates clean high quality spectograms with loads of additional features like collaging multiple spectograms and even applying various effects to these sound files. First time users may be confused by the vast features and are hence recommended to read the documentation once.

hey-bro-check-log - A CLI log checker maintained by Doujin Café Discord. Forked from the official hbcl repository.

CUETools - CUETools is a tool for lossless audio/CUE sheet format conversion. The goal is to make sure the album image is preserved accurately.

TuneMyMusic - Transfer your music library from any music service to any other you want. Supports Spotify, TIDAL, Apple Music, YouTube, Amazon Music, Pandora and many more.
TuneMyMusic by itself has some free-tier limits, using it to transfer your libary to Deezer via this partnership doesn't.
