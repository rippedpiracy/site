Movies & Shows

Some of these sites have Fake and Mirror sites, you should only use the ones we link to.
In case any of the sites are blocked or inaccessible, Change DNS to 1.1.1.1 or use GoodByeDPI / PowerTunnel.
We Strongly recommend you have uBlock Origin, Violentmonkey and bypass-all-shortlinks-debloated installed.

Streaming

Useful for watching Content easily and quickly, but mostly with a loss of Quality.

P-Stream - Spiritual successor of movie-web, it aggregates content from different providers and serves it in a modern, polished UX.

Fmovies+ | MovieOrca - Streaming sites with a UI similar to Prime Video, beware of Pop-ups.
There are some Anti-Popup filters in our Misc section.

WatchCartoonsOnline - Site for Cartoons and subbed/dubbed Anime.

StreamingCommunity - Constantly updated site with Italian Dubbed/Subbed Content, similar to Netflix.

Rarefilmm - Streaming site for classic, lost, and forgotten cinema.

Downloading

Higher Quality most of the time and better viewing experience compared to Streaming.
Enable File Explorer on Windows to display file extensions and make sure the filename ends in .mkv or .mp4. If ends in .exe it's Malware.

1337x (Torrent) - Torrent Tracker with an enourmous amount of Movies, TV Shows and everything in-between.
Quality can vary from one Release to another.

TorrentGalaxy (Torrent) - One of the better sources for new Movies and TV shows.

OlaMovies (DDL) - Site that has downloads for higher quality and relatively storage-friendly encodes, you can find Bollywood media here as well.

Pahe (DDL) - Has all sorts of content ranging from Anime to general Movies to Korean Drama.
You may have to disable FastForward to access the links.

Rarefilmm (DDL) - Site having downloads for classic, lost, and forgotten cinema. Also has a chatroom feature for requesting such films.

IRC

No Middle-Man between you, and Downloading the Content from the IRC bots. Requires an IRC Client such as HexChat.

MovieGods (XDCC) - A File sharing Network with a vast amount of resources, mainly for Movies & Shows. Head to #mg-chat to Download Content.

Apps

Stremio | Kodi - Clients for Streaming in general with an intuitive design, they both allow Streaming from Torrents/Magnet Links as well as from certain Sites and have a plethora of Addons available that will make your experience much better.
Check out r/StremioAddons and r/Addons4Kodi for a list of useful Addons for both clients.

qBittorrent | Deluge | Transmission - Free, Lightweight and open source Torrent Clients.
We do not recommend using µTorrent or BitTorrent, as it came bundled with Adware, Spyware, and Crypto-Miners in the past [1]. In its current state, it's Adware and Bloatware. We don't recommend using µTorrent v2.2.1 either due to confirmed Security Exploits.
        qBittorrent Enhanced Edition - A qBittorrent fork with enhanced privacy (IP filtering, auto-ban bots and copyright trolls, auto-update public trackers).
        qBittorrent Guide - A guide for configuring qBittorrent.

AB-DM | IDM | JD2 | Motrix | FDM - Download Managers that can boost Download speeds. Motrix and FDM support Torrenting, and JD2 is best for downloading multiple files simultaneously. IDM is paid.
        Elephant - Adds YT-DLP support to FDM, meaning it can download from all these sites.
        JDownloader 2 Guide - A guide for setting up JDownloader 2.

YT-DLP - Can download from over 1500 sites including: Crunchyroll, Funimation, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, PornHub and more. Link to all supported sites.
Maintained fork of the popular YouTube Downloader youtube-dl with more functionality.
Stacher - A modern YouTube Downloader, front-end/GUI for youtube-dl.
cobalt | community instances - Downloader that supports Instagram, Reddit, TikTok and more.
TubeDigger - Advanced Media Downloader, has wider support for capturing and downloading media.

DDL: Direct Download, where you can download a file from a server usually done through your Browser.

 

Torrent: A decentralised Peer-to-Peer file sharing protocol that is very common when sharing Pirated Content, since the file isnt on a single server that can easily be taken down.

 

XDCC: A computer file sharing method which uses the Internet Relay Chat network as a host service. In essence, it's a Direct Download.
