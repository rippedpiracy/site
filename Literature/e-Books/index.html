  • DeDRM - Tools to be used with Calibre to remove the DRM of e-Books from various retailers.
  • Bypass Paywalls Clean for Chrome & Firefox - Bypass News Article paywalls.
  • Unpaywall - When you view a research article, Unpaywall automatically looks for a copy in its index. If it finds one, click the green tab to read the article.
  • Archive Buttons | Remove Paywalls - Remove Paywalls from greedy News sources that ask for money to read their Articles.
